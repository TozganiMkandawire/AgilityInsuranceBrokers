THIRD PARTY INSURANCE...

Third-party insurance is a fundamental type of coverage that protects you against liability for damages or injuries you may cause to others while driving. It covers the cost of repairs or medical expenses for the other party involved in an accident, ensuring you comply with legal requirements and safeguard yourself from financial liability. While third-party insurance does not cover damage to your own vehicle, it provides essential protection against potentially significant expenses that could arise from accidents involving other vehicles, property, or individuals.
